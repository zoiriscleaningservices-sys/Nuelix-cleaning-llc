Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

At Nuelix Cleaning LLC ("we", "our", or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, www.nuelixcleaning.com, or use our cleaning services in Rhode Island.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you request an estimate, schedule a service, or contact us. This includes:

  • Contact Data: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing/property address.
  • Service Data: Details about your property, specific cleaning requirements, and access instructions (e.g., lockbox codes).
  • Communication Data: Records of communications, inquiries, and customer feedback.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect primarily to provide and improve our cleaning services. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Provide accurate estimates and quotes for cleaning services.
  • Schedule and fulfill residential, commercial, or post-construction cleanings.
  • Communicate with you regarding appointments, updates, and customer support.
  • Process payments and send invoices.

4. Third-Party Tools & Services

We utilize third-party tools to enhance your experience and process requests. By using our website, you acknowledge that your data may be processed by these tools in accordance with their respective privacy policies:

  • LeadConnector (HighLevel): Used for securely capturing your contact form submissions and managing our communications with you.
  • Elfsight: Used to display verified Google Reviews on our website.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, or unauthorized access. However, please note that no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by a revised "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed of how we are protecting your information.
